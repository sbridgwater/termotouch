Controlling when to automatically turn off the heating can save on the bills.

Awesome features

You can program up to 4 periods a day when the heating will be automatically turned on and off. You can turn it on before you wake up or off after you go to bed, on before you come home from work and off at times when you won't be at home.

Efficient and secure design

The control system on your LAN connects to the internet using a secure tunnel which filters ddos traffic before it gets to your home. The logon process requests a token from a cloud service and connects using https to the control system using this tunnel.
